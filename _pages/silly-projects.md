## [Slipland](https://rhzhang-ustc.github.io/slipland/)

A one-direction jumping game where surface friction determines your fate. Hop from platform to platform across six materials—ice slides, rubber grips, metal slips. Charge your jump with a press, release to launch; land too fast and you'll skid off. Each surface has its own friction range, sound, and feel. Low-friction (ice, metal) causes sliding; high-friction (rubber, sandpaper) is forgiving. Includes tactile feedback, wobble visuals, and haptic vibration on mobile.
